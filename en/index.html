Get to know me

My name is Maria Gabriela,
better known as Magaby

My name is María Gabriela, better known as Magaby... I won't go on for too long; I just want you to feel comfortable and confident. I have 20 years of experience as a hair stylist, working with all hair types, and for the last 12 years, I have focused exclusively on event styling. I love what I do; more than a job, it's my passion. Since I was a little girl, I discovered these skills within myself and always knew I wanted to style hair. Don't ask me how, I just knew it! Service is my calling. I love making people happy, especially brides, and I am thrilled to share special moments and be a part of them. Just as it is a special day for you, it is also special for me, because it allows me to experience and learn from a great variety of personalities. Every bride is a story, a style, and an essence.

A part of me that many don't know is that I studied Graphic Design alongside hair styling, an artistic skill that has been a great help and complement to my hairstyles. I discovered that styling, more than just doing hair, is a science of the arts: understanding anatomical proportion, color, shape, texture, morphopsychology, and layout is essential when creating a hairstyle. This, coupled with my conceptual artistic design background, brings a lot of functionality and harmony to my results.

I studied comprehensive hair styling and am certified by renowned brands such as Wella Professionals, Gama Italy, Ybera Paris, Nouvelle, and Novex.

Important Information

Preparation for
Day Zero

I

The Trial

It's essential to define your style. The trial prevents unwanted surprises and ensures the design fits your personality perfectly.

II

Proper Washing

Hair must be completely clean, washed ONLY with shampoo. No residue of styling creams, conditioner, wax, or masks.

III

Drying

It should be air-dried or recently blow-dried. Important: IT MUST NOT BE FLAT-IRONED under any circumstances before styling.

IV

Extensions

If your style requires dramatic volume, I will apply your extensions. You must provide them on the day of the event.
